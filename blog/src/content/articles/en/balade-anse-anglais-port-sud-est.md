This is one of the most beautiful crossings of Rodrigues — and one of the most demanding. The trail links Anse aux Anglais on the north coast to Port Sud-Est, cutting through the mountainous interior of the island.

## The highlight of the walk: the Victoire Dépret waterfall

Halfway along, you discover the Victoire Dépret waterfall, nestled in the hollow of a valley. It is one of the island's rare permanent water points — a refreshing stop before the second half of the route.

## Why a guide is mandatory

- The trail is not marked along its entire length
- **The elevation gain is significant** and some sections are technical
- After heavy rain, some sections can become difficult to cross
- A guide knows the alternative routes depending on the day's conditions

## Practical info

- **Duration:** 4 hours of actual walking
- **Difficulty:** hard — good physical condition required
- **Guide:** mandatory (book in advance)
- **Bring along:** hiking shoes, at least 2 L of water, snacks, rain possible
