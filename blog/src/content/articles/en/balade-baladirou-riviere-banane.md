This walk combines two of the pleasures of Rodrigues: coastal walking and snorkeling. The trail follows the coast from Baladirou to Rivière Banane, offering regular views over the turquoise lagoon.

## Snorkeling with Fanio

At Rivière Banane, **Fanio** organises snorkeling outings in the lagoon. His knowledge of the spots and the local marine life makes all the difference — he knows exactly where to find the best corals and observe the tropical fish. Ask around on site to arrange a session.

## Lunch under the thatched hut

A stone's throw from the beach, the Rivière Banane hut serves Creole dishes in a simple, authentic setting: grilled fish, achards, rice. The view over the lagoon does the rest.

## Practical info

- **No guide needed** for the walk
- **Closed shoes mandatory** — the trail is stony in places
- **Bring along:** mask, snorkel, fins (or rent on site), swimsuit, towel
- **For the snorkeling:** booking with Fanio recommended
