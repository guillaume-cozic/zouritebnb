This is probably the most iconic walk in Rodrigues — and the most accessible. The trail follows the east coast between Mourouk and Saint-François, crossing a succession of wild coves with turquoise waters.

## The route

Along the way, you discover in order:

- **Trou d'Argent** — probably the most famous, wedged between two limestone cliffs
- **Anse Bouteille** — more discreet, perfect for a swim
- **Anse Philibert** — a little further on, often deserted

The idea: **set off early in the morning** to enjoy each cove before the heat, and reach Saint-François in time for lunch.

## Where to eat in Saint-François

Two addresses are part of the walk:

- **Chez Robert et Solange** — family-style Creole cooking
- **Chez Dana** — the village's other go-to place

Book the day before if possible — seats are limited and guided groups often have lunch there.

## Practical info

- **No guide needed**
- **Difficulty:** easy, waymarked coastal trail
- **Bring along:** water, swimsuit, towel, sun protection, walking shoes
