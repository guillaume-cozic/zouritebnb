This is one of the most beautiful scenic roads on the island. The coastal road links **La Ferme**, on the south-west coast, to **Port Mathurin**, the capital, following the north-west shoreline of Rodrigues.

## What you will see

- **Cliffs and viewpoints** over the lagoon
- **Small fishing villages** clinging to the shoreline
- **Coves** where you can stop spontaneously for a swim
- **Views of the islets** in the distance

## When to go

**In the late afternoon**: the golden light on the sea is superb, and you reach Port Mathurin just in time for an aperitif — the moment when the capital comes alive.

## Practical info

- **To do by car** (or by scooter for the more adventurous)
- **Duration:** 1h30 to 2h with a few photo stops
- **Tip:** take your time — that is the spirit of the drive
