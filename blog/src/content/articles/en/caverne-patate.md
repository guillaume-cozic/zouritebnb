Caverne Patate is one of the most impressive underground cavities of Rodrigues. Carved into the coral limestone that forms part of the island, it extends over several hundred metres.

## The visit

The visit must be made with a guide, who presents the geological formation of the cave, its concretions (stalactites, stalagmites) and the surprising shapes that imagination lends to the rocks — hence the name of the place.

## Practical info

- **Guided visit mandatory**
- **Bring along:** closed shoes (the floor can be slippery), a lamp if needed
- **Good to know:** lighting inside is limited — the visit remains accessible but requires a minimum of mobility
