The Rodrigues lagoon is five to six times larger than the island itself — needless to say, it is worth the trip. **Moko** is one of the go-to skippers for exploring the southern islets.

## The Aquarium

The Aquarium is the nickname given to one of the finest snorkeling spots in the lagoon: shallow waters, well-preserved corals, a multitude of tropical fish. The name is well deserved — it really feels like swimming in a life-size natural pool.

## The southern islands

The outing usually includes a visit to one or more islets in the southern lagoon — deserted beaches, swimming, lunch on the sand depending on the package chosen.

## Options

- **Half day:** snorkeling at the Aquarium + one islet
- **Full day:** several islets, lunch included depending on the operator

## Practical info

- **Booking:** with Moko, to arrange in advance
- **Bring along:** swimsuit, towel, sun protection (water-resistant), hat, mask and snorkel (sometimes provided)
