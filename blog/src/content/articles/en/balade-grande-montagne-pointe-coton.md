This is **the** great crossing of Rodrigues. A full day of walking that strings together the endemic forest of Grande Montagne, the village of Gravier, the hamlet of Saint-François and the beach at Pointe Coton.

## The route

1. **Start: Grande Montagne** — in the heart of the nature reserve
2. **Gravier** — a small rural village with an authentic feel
3. **Saint-François** — possible lunch stop (at Robert and Solange's, or at Dana's)
4. **Finish: Pointe Coton** — a well-earned swim on one of the island's most beautiful beaches

## The bus back

From Pointe Coton, **take the bus** back up to Grande Montagne to collect the car. Ask about the timetable in the morning: buses are not frequent and the last departures can be early in the late afternoon.

## Practical info

- **Duration:** full day (allow 6 to 8 hours of walking including breaks)
- **Difficulty:** hard, significant elevation gain
- **Bring along:** hiking shoes, 2 L of water, snacks, a cap
- **Tip:** set off early in the morning to finish before the midday heat
