Rodrigues is a deeply Catholic island, and its **seven churches** are part of its living heritage. Each one tells a piece of the history of the inland villages — their construction, their evolution, the rhythm of the religious festivals that punctuate the year.

## Some stops along the way

- **Saint-Gabriel** — one of the largest churches on the island
- **Songes** — a small village chapel
- **Camp Pintade** — perched on the heights
- **Montagne Malgache** — emblematic of the interior

The full itinerary links these stops via the inland roads — a chance to pass through the villages, encounter everyday life and discover a Rodrigues very different from the coast.

## Practical info

- **To do by car**
- **No guide needed**
- **Good to know:** some churches are only open at service times — check with your host
- **A nice idea:** plan the visit for a Sunday morning to experience the atmosphere of a Rodriguan mass
