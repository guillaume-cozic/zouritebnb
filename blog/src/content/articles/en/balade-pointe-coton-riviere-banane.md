The trail linking Pointe Coton to Rivière Banane offers an alternative to the Mourouk → Saint-François walk for discovering the east coast of Rodrigues. More discreet, it remains less busy than the classics of the south.

## The route

The walk starts from the beach at **Pointe Coton**, one of the island's most beautiful beaches with its white sand and crystal-clear water. The trail then follows the coast northwards, alternating between clifftop sections and descents towards the coves.

Arriving at **Rivière Banane**, you can follow up with a snorkeling session (see the dedicated article) or lunch at the thatched hut.

## Practical info

- **No guide needed**
- **Difficulty:** easy, coastal trail
- **Closed shoes recommended**
- **Bring along:** water, swimsuit, sun protection
