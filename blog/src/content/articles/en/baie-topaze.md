Topaze Bay is one of the most peaceful bays on the south-west coast. Sheltered from the strong trade winds, it offers calm waters and an unspoilt setting — the perfect spot for a relaxing break, far from the bustle of Port Mathurin.

## What to do there

- **Swimming** in shallow waters
- **Strolling** along the bay
- **Watching** the surrounding mangrove

## Practical info

- **Free access, no guide needed**
- **Bring along:** swimsuit, towel, water, snacks
- **Good to know:** few facilities on site — bring what you need with you
