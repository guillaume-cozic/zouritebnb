At 398 metres above sea level, Mont Limon is the **highest point of Rodrigues**. From its summit, you take in the whole island at a glance: the north coast and the port of Mathurin, the hills of the interior and, on a clear day, the coral reef that fringes the lagoon.

## A short, accessible climb

Allow about **30 minutes** to reach the summit from the car park. The trail is short, waymarked and accessible to anyone in normal physical condition. It is an excellent introduction to get a feel for the island's geography in the first days of your stay.

## The best time to go

- **Early in the morning** to enjoy the soft light and avoid the heat
- **In the late afternoon** for the sunset over the lagoon

## Practical info

- **Duration:** 30 minutes round trip
- **No guide needed**
- **Bring along:** light walking shoes, water, a camera
